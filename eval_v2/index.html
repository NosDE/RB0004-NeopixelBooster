Performance of version2

This page provides evaluation of RB0004 – NeopixelBooster shown in the photo below:

You can power the strip with input voltage up to 18 V. The minimal supply voltage is 6 V, however, in such scenario the usage of the module makes no sense. The modules can be used with 2S Li-Ion cells (nominal voltage 8.4  V). Without an active cooling the step-down is able to deliver 4 A continuously on the output. Therefore, we do not recommend connecting more than 60 LEDs on a single module.

Measurement setup

The measurements above were taken in an ambient temperature 22°C using the following equipment:

  • Electronic load: B&K Precision 8540
  • Multimeter for voltage & current measurement: ESCORT 179
  • Oscilloscope: Rigol DS1054Z
  • Multimeter for contact temperature measurement: Uni-T UT-70A
